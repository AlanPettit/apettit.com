About

Alan Pettit is a Materials Science and Engineering graduate from the University of Toronto. He graduated with honours and with a minor in the field of engineering business.

Alan is currently in München, Germany, working towards a masters degree in Satellite Application Engineering at the Technical University of Munich.

Alan's project work has involved:

  • Characterization of diamond like carbon (DLC) coatings
  • Materials forensics and root cause failure analysis
